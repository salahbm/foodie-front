//FAQ
Welcome to our food discovery app! We hope you find it useful in discovering delicious restaurants in the area.
Below are some frequently asked questions about our app:

Q: How do I search for restaurants near my school?
A: You can search for restaurants in the app. It will automatically open some recommendations, and you can also browse by category.
And it will show you different varieties of restaurants nearby Dankook.

Q: Can restaurant owners apply for their restaurant?
A: Yes, they could; simply click on the settings and click on "Add New Restaurant." Then you will be directed to the restaurant.
Follow the application process on the application page.

Q: Will this show restaurants outside Dankook?
A: Note that our app is meant for Dankook University foods; therefore, for now, we don't support food outside the Dankook area (1-2km nearby).

Q: How do I create an account?
A: To create an account, simply click on the "Sign Up" button on the app's home screen and follow the prompts.

Q: Is the restaurant owner limited to restaurant applications?
A: No, restaurants have the right to change the information about their restaurants at any time, even deleting the input restaurant.
